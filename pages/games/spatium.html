Description

Spatium inter nos is our master's thesis game. This game is a 2.5D cooperative game in which you must cooperate with your partner to advance through the ship, covering yourself with lasers and solving puzzles in 0 gravity. All this with the restriction of being tied with a laser rope.

My role

I have been one of the members of the design department in charge of level design, mechanics, boss artificial intelligence, menu flow and mechanics prototyping in blueprins, among other things. The UE4 engine has been used to develop the project.
