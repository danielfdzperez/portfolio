Description

Crazy Bubble is a casual game, whose main mechanic is to spin the phone and try to bounce off the walls as many times as possible to get the best score. To make the game more difficult, there are springs that give speed to the player's movement, while increasing the score received with each bounce. There are also spikes that when touched make you roll, unless you have a shield.

I have designed and programmed the game.
