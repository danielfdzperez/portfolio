Description

It is a platform puzzle game, where the player must maintain his temperature. The player can lose or gain temperature by touching the ground and jumping. This game has been made for game jam Mini Jam 73: Power, with the limitation to use only 5 colors.

I have designed and programmed the game.
This game is under development.
