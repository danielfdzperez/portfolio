Description

Denbora is a 3D time travel puzzle game based on portal, just like portal it has a strong narrative, in this case a criticism of the current labor and economic system. To overcome the levels the player must use the elements that are in the level and 2 different types of time travel, a normal one in which you stay in your position and another in which the destination position is the last one from which you traveled in the time. The player must play with these 2 ways of traveling and with the restriction that objects moving in the present affect objects in the future.
